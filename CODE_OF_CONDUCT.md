# Code of Conduct

## The short version

Be kind. Assume good faith. Keep the project a welcoming space for everyone
regardless of background or experience level.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful in issues, pull requests, and review comments.
- Offer feedback on the work, not the person.
- When you disagree, explain your reasoning and listen to the other side.
- Ask before making assumptions about someone's intent.

## Unacceptable behavior

Personal attacks, sustained disruption of discussion, or any conduct a
reasonable person would consider disrespectful or unwelcoming has no place
here.

## Scope

This applies to all project spaces — the repository, issues, pull requests,
discussions, and any other venue where contributors are interacting about
the project.

## Enforcement

Report issues by emailing **jake@costa.security**. Reports will be handled
confidentially. Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or restrict
project access in response to behavior that violates this policy.
